Key terms

  • Agentic Gemini era
    A periodization claim: May 2026 is the inflection point where Google's AI shifts from answering questions to taking multi-step actions on the user's behalf across Google surfaces
  • Proactive Long-Running & Managed Agents
    Software that initiates actions in the background without explicit prompting — managing inbox, scheduling, monitoring information, suggesting actions
  • Gemini Intelligence
    A platform-level capability layer baked into Android/Googlebook that interprets context and acts — not a single model but a brand for embedded agentic features
  • Information agents
    Background processes inside Search that monitor topics on the user's behalf and surface updates with links/actions
  • Antigravity
    Google's agentic coding capability, here surfaced inside Search to generate UI and interactive visuals on demand
  • Generative UI
    Search-rendered interfaces (dashboards, mini-apps) constructed on the fly per query rather than retrieved from indexed pages
  • Universal Cart Agentic Commerce
    Cross-merchant, cross-surface shopping cart that follows the user through Search, Gemini, YouTube, Gmail
  • Googlebook
    A laptop category built ground-up for Gemini, manufactured by Acer/Asus/Dell/HP/Lenovo — the AI-PC analogue Google now controls the reference design for
  • Android Halo
    An OS-level surface for monitoring agent progress without context-switching

Arguments

Google is the natural leader of the agentic AI era. strength: low
Explicit premises:
  • Google has invested in ML/AI for 20+ years.
  • Gemini 3.5 is frontier intelligence.
  • Gemini Omni unifies modalities.
  • Agents now run across Search, Android, Googlebook, eyewear, cars.
Implicit premises:
  • Distribution and surface integration matter more than raw model quality once frontier capability is achieved.
  • Users will prefer agents that live in apps they already use over standalone agent products.
  • The agentic shift is a platform shift, and platform shifts favor incumbents with hardware + OS + browser + identity.
Inferences:
  • Standalone agent vendors (OpenAI's ChatGPT agents, Anthropic's Claude, Perplexity) are at a structural disadvantage on consumer surfaces Google owns.
  • The open web is being repositioned as raw material for Google-rendered generative UI rather than the destination of a search click.
Search is not being disrupted — it is being upgraded.
Explicit premises:
  • Information agents now run inside Search 24/7.
  • Antigravity brings agentic coding into Search.
  • Generative UI renders custom dashboards and mini-apps inside Search.
  • The Search box itself is being rebuilt for the first time in 25 years.
Implicit premises:
  • Users will continue to start in a Google-controlled surface rather than a chat interface.
  • Advertising and commerce can be preserved in a generative-UI world via Universal Cart.
  • Publishers will continue to supply content even as the click-through model collapses.
Inferences:
  • The economic relationship between Google and the open web is being unilaterally rewritten in May 2026.
  • Publisher payment / licensing tensions will intensify but are not addressed here.
Proactive, ambient AI is the right product direction. strength: low
Explicit premises:
  • The Gemini app is becoming a proactive 24/7 partner.
  • Android Halo lets agents work without interrupting flow.
  • Gemini Intelligence on phones proactively suggests actions.
Implicit premises:
  • Users want software that initiates action on their behalf.
  • Trust in delegated action will scale with capability.
  • Background-acting agents will not create accountability or privacy crises that outweigh their convenience.
Inferences:
  • Failure modes of proactive agents (wrong calendar entry, wrong purchase, leaked email) are not discussed — but they will define the next 12 months of public debate.

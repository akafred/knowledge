Key terms

  • Open Space
    A self-organizing conference format in which participants propose and run the sessions, producing many parallel, agenda-free threads (Harrison Owen's method, which Fowler documents in a separate bliki entry)
  • Chatham House Rule
    A confidentiality convention under which attendees may use what was said but may not reveal who said it, unless the speaker gives permission — invoked here to keep comments unattributed
  • fragments
    Fowler's practice of publishing short, standalone insight posts over time rather than assembling them into one long, coherent article

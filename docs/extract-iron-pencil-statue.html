Arguments

You should stop waiting for ideal conditions and act now. strength: medium
Explicit premises:
  • You are waiting for ideal conditions.
  • Ideal conditions do not exist and will never happen.
Implicit premises:
  • Waiting for something impossible is irrational.
  • The desired action has value worth pursuing under imperfect conditions.
Inferences:
  • Since ideal conditions will never arrive, continued waiting guarantees inaction.

Open-Weight Models

Models whose weights are publicly released and can be run or fine-tuned on any infrastructure — increasingly sufficient for a large share of real work, with open governance questions.

Also known as: open-weight models, open weight, open-weight, Open-weight models, open weight / open source (AI), open models / hosted open models, open models, open-source models, openwashing, engine vs. car, vegan models

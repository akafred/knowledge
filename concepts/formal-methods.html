Formal Methods

Practical techniques for precisely specifying and checking system behaviour — specification languages (TLA+, Quint), model checking, model-based testing, trace validation — recast from academic discipline into an accessible, AI-assisted tool for reasoning about edge cases of complex distributed/concurrent systems and asserting correctness of increasingly AI-generated code.

Also known as: formal methods, formal specification, formal verification, formal model, model checking, model-based testing, trace validation, specification language, TLA+, Quint

Part of: Verifiability (as the Quality Bar)

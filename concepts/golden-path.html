Golden Path

Making the right way the easy way — paved, opinionated defaults so good that adoption runs on intrinsic motivation rather than mandate.

Also known as: golden path, Golden path, golden path / kul-luxverk, paved roads, Paved roads, Golden brick, golden bricks, gjøre det lett og rett (lett og rett), gjøre det lett og rett, lett og rett, make it easy / don't shame, engineering enablement

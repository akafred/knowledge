AI Backlash

The organized and organic public revulsion against generative AI's externalized harms, and the calibrated-skepticism discourse around it.

Also known as: backlash, Backlash, QuitGPT, net negative, fear-based marketing, doomer industrial complex, unbeliever contributions, counterpoints to techno-optimism

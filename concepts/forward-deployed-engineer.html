Forward-Deployed Engineer

Engineers embedded in customer environments to make AI products deliver value — the human integration layer AI labs are scaling because organizational absorption, not model capability, is the bottleneck.

Also known as: forward-deployed engineer, forward-deployed engineer (FDE), Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE), FDE, forward-deployed, Deployment Company (OpenAI Deployment Company), AI transformation

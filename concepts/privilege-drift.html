Privilege Drift / Scope Sprawl

The gradual, seemingly-reasonable accumulation of an AI agent's permissions beyond its original scope and purpose over time — each individual grant justified in isolation, but the aggregate expanding the attack surface and violating least privilege; amplified when multiple agents share resources in a multi-agent system.

Also known as: privilege drift, scope sprawl, permission accumulation, over-permissioning, permission creep

Part of: Agent Governance (Internal)

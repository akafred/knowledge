Leader Restraint

The discipline of withholding the leader's own answer to create space for the team to find one — leading without the answer key.

Also known as: når sjefen har svaret, tier resten, fasiten, ta plass / la andre få plass, supporting character, lede uten fasit, marsj-ordre (vs. retning)

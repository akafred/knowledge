Jagged Capability (On Rails / Off Rails)

Karpathy's framing of LLM competence as domain-dependent and extreme — brilliant, PhD-level performance in verifiable, RL-optimized domains ("on rails") coexisting with basic failures elsewhere ("off rails") — a variance far more extreme than in humans.

Also known as: jaggedness, on rails / off rails

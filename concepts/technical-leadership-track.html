Technical Leadership Track (IC / Staff-Plus Ladder)

The staff to principal to distinguished-engineer / architect career ladder where one leads through technical judgment, design authority and influence rather than personnel management — parallel to the management ladder, with no direct reports.

Also known as: staff engineer, principal engineer, IC leadership ladder, technical track, individual-contributor track, staff-plus, dual ladder, leadership not management, title-less technical leadership

Part of: Technology Leadership (Heis)

Meta-Skills & Contextual Judgment

The transferable ability to read context, judge which practices fit, and run small experiments — the durable skill layer above any specific tool or method.

Also known as: meta-skill, meta-skills, four buckets, process mechanics of leading, irreplaceable (with AI), the smallest experiment, Thinkies, metacognition

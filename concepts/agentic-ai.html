Agentic AI

AI systems that independently plan, decide and use tools across multiple steps to complete tasks — distinct from chatbots and single-shot assistants.

Also known as: agentic AI, agentic ai, AI agents, agent, agents, AI agent, agent / agentic workload, Agent (Anthropic's enterprise definition), agentic shift, agentic AI (Schrage), autonomous agents

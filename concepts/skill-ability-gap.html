Skill–Ability Gap

The historically-novel decoupling of ability (capacity to effect an outcome, supplied on demand by tools and AI) from skill (mastery earned through practice), which also strips away the moral and professional norms skill-acquisition used to bundle in — collapsing the barrier to harmful action for norm-free outsiders.

Also known as: gap between skill and ability, skill vs ability, ability without skill, capability without mastery, script kiddie, lowered barrier to entry, lowers the barrier to entry

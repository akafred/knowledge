Extended Thinking / Reasoning

The internal reasoning models perform before output — structurally load-bearing for complex autonomous work, expensive to serve, and contested when providers reduce it.

Also known as: extended thinking / thinking tokens / thinking depth, extended thinking, thinking tokens, thinking depth, reasoning models, chain-of-thought, redact-thinking / thinking redaction

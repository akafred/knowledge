Differential Diagnosis (Diagnostic Reasoning)

Isolating root cause by comparing across cases/populations, separating conflated phenomena, and distinguishing symptom from cause — building a complete labeled dataset over the whole failure population rather than analysing individual cases more deeply.

Also known as: differential diagnosis, diagnostic reasoning, root-cause isolation, population-level debugging, separating conflated failures, symptom vs root cause

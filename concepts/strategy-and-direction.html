Strategy & Direction-Setting

Strategy as deliberate deselection plus a concrete, personally believed narrative — compressed enough that a distributed organization can act on it.

Also known as: strategisk narrativ (strategic narrative), strategic narrative, Retning, retning, Strategi på en ølbrikke, Velge bort, velge bort, Must-win battles, Tydelig, Skråsikker, company bets, reality distortion field, Henge sammen / få det til å henge sammen, Forankring

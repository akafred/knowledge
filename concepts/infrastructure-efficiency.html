Documents (2)

  • Meta Deploys Unified AI Agents to Automate Performance Optimization at Hyperscale Craig Risi · 2026-05-18 Reader 🖥️
    Meta has built a unified, LLM-agent-based capacity-efficiency platform that encodes senior engineering expertise as reusable 'skills' and standardized 'tools' to autonomously detect and fix performance and resource inefficiencies across its hyperscale infrastructure, exemplifying an industry-wide shift from reactive, manual performance tuning to continuous, real-time, self-optimizing systems.
  • Presentation: How Netflix Shapes our Fleet for Efficiency and Reliability Joseph Lynch, Argha C · 2026-04-20 Reader 🖥️
    Efficiency and reliability are not opposing goals but a single optimization problem: by reasoning about risk-adjusted cost (not utilization) and managing the balance of hardware supply against software demand through layered proactive and reactive levers, you can run critical services with high reliability while spending less overall.

Team Autonomy

Teams with real mandate, competence and decision rights inside explicit boundaries — mission-based direction with local freedom over the how.

Also known as: autonomi, autonomous teams, empowered teams, bemyndigete team (empowered teams), self-managed teams (selvledelse), selvledelse, Sirkler av autonomi, Oppdragsbasert ledelse, mission-based leadership, tight/loose ledelse (Rune Ulvnes' framework), tight/loose, structured empowerment

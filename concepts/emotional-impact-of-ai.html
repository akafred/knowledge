Emotional Impact of AI Work

The psychological states AI adoption induces in practitioners and leaders — alienation, dulling, rollercoaster highs and lows, anxious FOMO and delusions of productivity.

Also known as: følelser, fremmedgjøring, alienation, sløv, berg- og dalbane, AI psychosis, AI Psychosis, excited anxiety, itchy brain

  • implicitThe proper unit of analysis for AI's impact on software development is the developer's feelings (følelser), not productivity metrics.
    KI, utvikling og følelser
  • explicitThe way out of alienation is partly technical (workshops, hand-coding the foundation, TDD that forces the agent to stop between steps) and partly psychological (reframing the job as problem-solving, not programming).
    KI, utvikling og følelser
  • explicitClassical engineering practices (TDD, DDD, agile, continuous delivery, simple refactored code) are simultaneously (a) what produces the kind of code agents themselves work best with and (b) the developer's existential anchor against alienation.
    KI, utvikling og følelser
  • psychological / normativeThe durable motivation for founders and makers is the maker-compulsion itself — the 'itchy brain' / wanting to make and connect with people — not external validation, which 'cooks' you over time.

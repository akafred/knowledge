AI-Native Company

A company designed from inception so machine intelligence participates in ordinary business work — an operating model, not a product category.

Also known as: AI-native, AI-native startup, AI-native company, expert loops

Documents (2)

  • The Founder's Playbook: Building An AI-Native Startup Claude (Anthropic) · 2026-05-05 source ↗ Reader 🖥️
    Because AI now collapses the cost of building, researching, and operating a company, the AI-native founder's job is no longer execution but orchestration of AI across four stages (Idea, MVP, Launch, Scale), and a small (often single-founder) team that systematically routes work through Claude, Claude Cowork, and Claude Code can reach outcomes that previously required a fully-staffed organisation.
  • #3: How to Build an AI-Native Startup from Day One Will Schenk (TheFocus.AI) and Ksenia Se (Turing Post) · 2026-04-11 source ↗ Reader 🖥️
    An AI-native startup is one designed from inception so that machine intelligence can participate in ordinary business work; achieving this requires making organizational knowledge machine-legible, choosing tools for visibility and portability, and restructuring work around outcomes rather than handoffs -- shifting the founder question from 'where do we use AI?' to 'what parts of the business assume intelligence is abundant, uneven, and improvable?'

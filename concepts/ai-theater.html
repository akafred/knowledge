AI Confidence Theater

Performative projection of AI mastery — inflating modest, unreliable wins into transformation claims — which corrodes trust and obscures where real value lives.

Also known as: AI Confidence Theater, confidence theater, Status theater, status theater, reverse-hustle hustle culture, fake baseline, the next thousand prompts

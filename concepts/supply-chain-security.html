Software Supply-Chain Security

Protecting the integrity of software dependencies and artifacts against compromise via maintainers, registries and build pipelines — trust replaced by verification.

Also known as: supply chain attack, supply-chain attack, software supply chain security, supply chain risk, provenance, provenance attestation, SBOM, Software Bill of Materials (SBOM), chain of custody, verification (over trust), transferable maintainership, dependency confusion, transitive dependencies, social engineering, RAT (Remote Access Trojan), Cyber Resilience Act, EU Cyber Resilience Act (CRA)

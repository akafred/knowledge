Insourcing & In-House Capability

Repatriating the people, decisions and problem-solving capability an organization depends on — because using a vendor's AI is not the same as having the capability.

Also known as: Insourcing (hjemtaking), insourcing, hjemtaking, Hjemtaking av jurister, outsourcet problemløsning, in-house capability, innovation sovereignty, modningsreise (maturation journey), modningsreise, Business case for insourcing, capability mismatch, hollowed out

  • How Chinese Firms Are Saving Western Brands Shameen Prashantham · 2026-06 Reader 🖥️
    Western firms whose brand strength has outrun their in-house manufacturing, design, and digital capabilities should consider deliberate divestment — often to Chinese acquirers whose vertically integrated, patient, light-touch playbook can revive the brand — because clinging to ownership without the underlying capabilities is now the greater strategic risk.
  • 16) Inga Strümke om hvor AI er på vei, den outsourcede problemløsningen og datamanifolden Truls Jørgensen og Audun Fauchald Strand — Heis Podcast · 2026-05-04 source ↗ Reader 🖥️
    Norway's understanding of AI has been hijacked by the chatbot — both as the dominant interface and as the dominant mental model — and this collapses our maturation path: we substitute buying foreign LLM products for the slower, harder work of in-house machine-learning, problem framing, and explainability, even though the technical theory (data manifolds, approximation, neurosymbolic combinations) and the geopolitics both demand that we develop our own capability rather than outsource our problem-solving.
  • 7) Stine Haugseth: Insourcing, retning og strategi på en ølbrikke Truls Jørgensen og Audun Fauchald Strand — Heis Podcast · 2025-12-15 source ↗ Reader 🖥️
    Insourcing, retning, and strategi-på-en-ølbrikke are three faces of one leadership move: pulling the locus of decision-making in-house and then compressing direction tightly enough that a distributed organisation can actually act on it — insourcing repatriates the people who must own the work, oppdragsbasert ledelse and sirkler av autonomi give those people a clear mission with explicit frihetsgrenser, and the ølbrikke forces utviklingsstrategi down to the few hovedområder a development organisation can actually carry, with OKRs underneath and an explicit business case underneath that.

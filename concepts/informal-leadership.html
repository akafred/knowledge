Informal Leadership

Leadership as an activity and process rather than a title — influence through voice, delivery and credibility without formal mandate.

Also known as: uformell ledelse / leder uten tittel, uformell leder, leder uten tittel, ledelse uten personalansvar, å være leder vs. å drive med ledelse, title-less leadership, ledelse er en prosess (ikke en person), rolle vs person

Functions to (a) coordinate the gruppe and (b) represent the gruppe to ekstern parties (the talsperson). In modern organisations a formal leder is appointed from outside; this is 'unaturlig' (not 'wrong,' but not evolutionary). Truls Jørgensen og Audun Fauchald Strand — Heis Podcast · 2026-06-01		rolle vs personOnce you reach a senior level, you are not there as a person but as a rolle. The rolle's shape is determined by the constellation of forventninger from everyone with a stake (not by the person hiring you), and shifts on the day you arrive — and continually thereafter as kontekst changes. The personal task is to find or grow into a rolle one can sustain over time without being stressed apart by it; this requires tålmodighet. Failure of fit does not mean the person is elendig, only that råd-marginen was too small. Counter-illustration: Trump as 'too much person, too little role.'

Writing as Thinking

Writing as the struggle through which beliefs, judgment and self are formed — outsourcing it to AI outsources the thinking, risking convergence of style and thought.

Also known as: Writing, writing is thinking, Cognitive struggle, cognitive struggle, Linguistic convergence (at civilizational scale), linguistic convergence, Co-creator, Self

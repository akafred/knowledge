Hooks

Deterministic, system-level automations triggered at fixed points in an agent's lifecycle, used to enforce behavior rather than relying on the model to remember.

Also known as: hooks, hook, stop hook / stop-phrase-guard, stop hook, Skills / MCPs / hooks

Part of: Agent Harness

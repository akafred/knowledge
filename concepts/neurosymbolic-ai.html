Neurosymbolic AI

Architectures combining neural networks with deterministic symbolic logic — argued to be the real substrate of working agent products like Claude Code.

Also known as: Neurosymbolic AI, neurosymbolic, nevrosymbolsk AI (neurosymbolic AI), nevrosymbolsk AI, classical symbolic AI, deterministisk vs probabilistisk

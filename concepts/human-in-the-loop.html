Human-in-the-Loop

Workflow designs that keep a human exercising judgment at defined checkpoints while agents execute autonomously in between.

Also known as: human-in-the-loop, HITL, judgment point, human approval, pause in place, AFK (away-from-keyboard) task vs. human-in-the-loop task, day shift / night shift, approval fatigue, two-stage classification

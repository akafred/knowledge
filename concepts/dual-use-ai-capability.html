Dual-Use AI Capability (Offense-Defense Parity)

AI amplifies cyber-offense and cyber-defense using the same underlying capability, so improvements that help defenders equally help attackers; because the knowledge is inseparable and open-weight local models evade guardrails, offensive capability democratizes to actors without the skill or ethical formation that once gated it, and the durable response is to harness AI for defense rather than restrict the models.

Also known as: dual-use AI, dual-use capability, offense-defense parity, offense and defense inseparable, same knowledge serves attack and defense, democratizing offensive capability, skill-ability decoupling

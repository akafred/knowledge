Vendor Self-Reported Metrics

Vendor blog posts presenting product adoption/impact via self-reported, methodology-free metrics as if they were independently verified proof — a recurring PR genre across product domains (not limited to social-good/humanitarian framing).

Also known as: self-reported metrics, unverified vendor claims, vendor telemetry

  • incompleteThe piece attributes Gemini's growth entirely to demographic tailwinds and language quality, without considering obvious confounds: Gemini's free pricing tier, default placement/bundling on Android devices, reduced availability of comparably fluent local-language competitors, or marketing/notification spend in the region.

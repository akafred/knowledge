Technology Leadership (Heis)

Leadership that moves fluently between organizational floors — executive bridge and delivery engine room — translating between domains while staying credibly connected to the craft.

Also known as: teknologiledelse (technology leadership), teknologiledelse, Heis, heis (the elevator), kunsten å ta heis (the art of taking the elevator), heis-fører (elevator operator), ulike etasjer (different floors), bro og maskinrom (bridge and engine room), T-ledelse (T-leadership), teammodellen (the team model)
